FAQs

If you have any additional questions, please email me: studio.aprilshowers2021@gmail.com!

Most earrings are made of polymer clay. However some earrings are also made with glass beads, pearls, or UV resin. All of my earrings are hypoallergenic, nickel-free, and are either gold plated or surgical steel. Please read the specific item details on Etsy to find out the precise materials used in each earring.

Earrings are durable, but still can be broken. Avoid dropping or bending earrings. Avoid wearing earrings to sleep or in the shower. Clean with alcohol wipes or a damp towel. Store in a dry, cool spot.

All polymer clay earrings are extremely lightweight! The largest pairs are about 4 grams which is less than the weight of one quarter but most pairs weight even less. Earring sizes should be in the listing description. If you are not finding the exact details you are looking for, feel free to contact me.

I do accept custom or personalized orders. If you are interested, please contact me through Etsy or by email. I am excited to work with you!

Most of my earrings are one or few of a kind. I will sometimes remake earring styles, but even then, every pair is unique and special. Also some earrings that I make are specifically for in-person events. Please contact me if you would like me to recreate a old style.

I accept wholesale orders. Please contact me for more information about wholesale orders and for my wholesale catalog.

Yes I offer free shipping on all Etsy orders! If you live in the Berkeley, CA area I am also able to do local pickups and drop offs. Message me and we can work something out.
