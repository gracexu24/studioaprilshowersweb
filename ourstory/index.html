Meet the Maker!

Grace Xu

Owner + Artist

Hi! My name is Grace, and I am a high school junior and the artist behind Studio April Showers. Creating art has always been something that I have loved to do. I draw inspiration from many subjects including nature, food, art and anything that catches my eye. I'm also a dancer and passionate cat lover.

Studio April Shower's Story

Established in 2020, Studio April Showers was born amidst a global pandemic. It was a wonderful new beginning for me during such a strange time. Much like the saying “April showers bring May flowers,“ this shop was the good that grew from hard times. With Studio April Showers, I aim to take the joy I experience through my work and share it with others. I make all of my pieces to be unique and long-lasting, and many are one-of-a-kind. They come in a wide range of styles, shapes, and sizes, and I hope that there will be a piece that is perfect for you and will bring you happiness. If you have any questions or want to make a special inquiry, please contact me via Etsy (StudioAprilShowers), Instagram (studio.aprilshowers), or email(studio.aprilshowers2021@gmail.com).
