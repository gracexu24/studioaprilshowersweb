Swimming Pools

Summer Special

These are my favorite summer earrings that I started making the summer of 2021. Each year these come back with new iterations, different pool shapes, new swimmers and more. These limited addtion earrings are some of my favorite but also most time consuming earrings to make. The "water" effect is achieved through a mix of translucent and opaque clay. Then each little swimmer is individually handmade with tiny bits of polymer clay. To finish everything off and give it its shinny appearance, I add a coat of glossy UV resin. Acrylic Space Kitties

May 2022

While I use polymer clay as my main material in most of my earrings, I also experiment with different mediums such as beads, resin, wire, and more. These earrings are made out of iridescent acrylic. My friend helped me desgin the image, I drew it on illustrator, and then with the help of my teacher, I used my school's laser cutter to etch and cut these earrings.
